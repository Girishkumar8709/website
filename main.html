Why AI CERTs?

Our certifications are vendor agnostic, ensuring a broad and versatile skill set. Each certification is meticulously designed to focus on real-world roles and responsibilities, guaranteeing practical, job-ready expertise.

Our diverse certifications validate skills across all technical, business, and future-oriented roles, equipping every team to thrive in the AI era.

At the forefront of innovation, AI CERTs™ uniquely integrates AI with Blockchain technology in our program designs, setting us apart as the premier certification entity in tech education.

AI CERTs™ is dedicated to cultivating a future-ready, tech-empowered world by aiming to certify a billion people, while also committing 10% of yearly profits to building schools for underprivileged children.

We're transforming online testing with Proctoring 365™, using secure, AI-powered exam proctoring to streamline the process and ensure test integrity.

Our seasoned veterans in AI, Blockchain, and education craft high-impact programs for your workforce, leveraging their unmatched knowledge and experience.

With access to a vast network of over 1000 subject matter experts specializing in AI and Blockchain, our certifications are consistently enriched with the latest industry insights for relevance and up-to-date knowledge.
